OOP - Abstracting & Naming Concepts: Level 3

It is mid 2000s now, through constant innovation and your disciplined software development, the company is now firmly established as the topmost telecom service provider. They now plan to introduce packs(e.g. std pack, data pack). These packs could be of three types:

  • Free data/minutes/sms upto a certain limit
  • Reduced data/minutes/sms rates
  • Free data/minutes/sms upto a certain limit and reduced rates post free usage limit
